Features

Everything a home cook needs

Chefly isn't just a recipe finder — it's your complete kitchen companion.

🤖
Claude AI Powered
Backed by Anthropic's Claude — one of the world's most capable AI models — for creative, realistic recipe suggestions every time.
🔥
Nutrition Tracking
Every recipe shows estimated calories and protein per serving. Scale up or down with our serving size adjuster.

Toggle Healthy Mode to get low-calorie, high-protein recipes. Perfect for fitness goals without sacrificing flavour.
