Our Story

We believe everyone deserves to cook great food

Chefly was built to solve a universal problem — you have ingredients, you're hungry, but you have no idea what to make. We fixed that with AI.

Our Mission

Ending the "what's for dinner?" question forever

Every day, millions of people stand in their kitchen, open the fridge, and have absolutely no idea what to cook. They either order takeaway or make the same boring pasta again.

Chefly changes that. By combining the power of Claude AI with a beautifully simple interface, we turn any combination of ingredients into 5 creative, personalised recipes — in seconds, no sign-up required.

Our values

Built With

The technology behind Chefly

We chose the best tools available to build a fast, private, and intelligent cooking assistant.

🤖
Anthropic Claude AI
Claude Sonnet powers all recipe generation and step-by-step cooking guides. It's one of the world's most capable and safety-focused AI models, giving Chefly genuinely creative, realistic recipe ideas.
🎨
Pure HTML, CSS & JavaScript
No frameworks, no build tools, no dependencies. Chefly is a handcrafted single-file web app — it loads instantly, works offline-ready, and runs on any device with a browser.
📦
localStorage
Your API key and saved cookbook recipes live entirely in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server except your direct API calls to Anthropic. Complete privacy, zero backend.
🎬
YouTube Search Integration
Every recipe links to a tailored YouTube search so you can watch a cooking tutorial for the exact dish. No scraping, no API — just a direct, smart search link generated by AI.
